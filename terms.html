Terms of Service

Use YUVA Club to learn, lead, and inspire.

These terms apply to students, parents, organization administrators, and platform administrators.

Legal review note: This is a production draft based on the current platform. Final legal clauses such as governing law, dispute resolution, liability limits, and consumer rights require review by qualified counsel.

Acceptance and Purpose

By using YUVA Club, creating an account, registering a student, activating a parent account, accepting an organization invitation, or accessing an administrator dashboard, you agree to these Terms.

YUVA Club supports youth leadership development through student registration, presentation practice, topic selection, research submission, AI-assisted feedback, parent visibility, organization participation, certificates, volunteer-hour tracking, and administrative oversight.

Eligibility and Account Types

The current registration workflow is designed for students ages 13 to 21 and requires parent or guardian information and parent permission acknowledgement.

YUVA Club currently supports student accounts, parent accounts linked to student records, invitation-only organization administrator accounts, and one platform Master Admin account initially assigned to admin@yuvaclub.app.

Legal review note: Final age eligibility and verified consent requirements should be reviewed before broad launch.

Account Security

Users are responsible for keeping login credentials secure. Users may not share passwords, impersonate others, bypass permissions, access another user's account, or attempt to view records outside their assigned role.

YUVA Club may suspend, deactivate, or restrict access when needed for security, safety, abuse prevention, or platform operations.

Student Ownership and Organization Membership

The student owns the global YUVA identity, portfolio, certificates, presentation history, volunteer history, and learning records associated with that account.

Organizations own only organization-specific membership, assignments, groups, coaches, teachers, moderators, organization activities, and organization-specific permissions. Removing a student from an organization does not delete the student's global YUVA account or historical achievement records.

Acceptable Use

  • Do not upload harmful, illegal, abusive, hateful, sexually explicit, exploitative, unsafe, infringing, or malicious content.
  • Do not harass, threaten, shame, bully, impersonate, or misrepresent identity, age, parent authorization, organization affiliation, or achievements.
  • Do not bypass login, role, organization, CSRF, or security controls.
  • Do not interfere with the platform or another user's learning environment.
  • Do not misuse AI feedback to harm, shame, mislead, or unfairly judge students.

Student Content and AI Feedback

Students may submit topics, research notes, sources, presentation outlines, prepared questions, and uploaded files. YUVA Club may use student submissions to provide platform features, administrator review, AI-assisted feedback, certificates, portfolio summaries, and organization-specific learning support.

AI feedback is informational and educational. It is not a substitute for adult judgment, mentor review, or platform administrator approval.

Legal review note: Confirm AI vendor terms, disclosure, and data handling before final publication.

Presentations, Recordings, and Events

Students may participate in presentations, live sessions, or online events. The registration form includes acknowledgement that sessions may be recorded for educational purposes. The application includes protected Zoom meeting fields, but automated recording storage is not currently implemented in the codebase.

Legal review note: Recording terms require legal review before publication.

Certificates, Badges, Portfolio, and Volunteer Hours

YUVA Club may display or generate certificates, badges, ranks, presentation counts, volunteer or service hours, portfolio summaries, and progress information. These records may require administrator review. YUVA Club may correct errors, revoke improperly issued certificates, or update records when needed to maintain accuracy.

Organization and Parent Rules

Organization administrators may access only records assigned to their organization. They may not create Master Admins, change platform-wide settings, edit global content, access platform billing, manage global AI settings, or view records outside their assigned organization.

Parent access is limited to students linked to that parent account. Parents may not attempt to access another student's records.

Communications, Privacy, and Third-Party Services

YUVA Club may send service-related emails, including registration notifications, account activation links, invitation links, password reset links, support messages, and administrative notifications.

Use of YUVA Club is also governed by the Privacy Policy, Parent & Student Consent Policy, and Child Safety & Community Guidelines.

YUVA Club may rely on third-party services for hosting, database storage, email, AI-assisted feedback, and online meetings. Current implementation references Azure hosting, Azure SQL support, OpenAI API configuration, and Zoom meeting fields.

No Professional Advice and Platform Availability

YUVA Club provides educational leadership practice and learning support. It does not provide legal, medical, mental health, financial, or professional advice.

YUVA Club may change, suspend, or discontinue features and may suspend or terminate access for violations, safety issues, organization suspension, or misuse.
