What Works on Mobile

YUVA Club is currently a secure installable web app. It is designed for mobile browsers and home-screen access while native App Store and Google Play apps remain a future option.

Student Access

Students can register, sign in, view their dashboard, select topics, submit research, see Zoom details, and track progress.

Parent Access

Parents can sign in from mobile browsers to view linked student progress and account information.

Organization Access

Organization administrators can use the secure admin portal to invite and manage students assigned to their organization.

Master Admin Access

Platform administration remains protected behind the admin login and role checks.

Home Screen Install

Android and iPhone users can add YUVA Club to the home screen for app-like access.

Responsive Design

Public pages and portals are designed to work across phone, tablet, laptop, and desktop screens.

Install Guide

Add YUVA Club to Your Home Screen

Use the platform directly in your browser, or install it as a home-screen app for faster access.

Android
  1. Open yuvaclub.app in Chrome, Edge, or Samsung Internet.
  2. Tap the browser menu.
  3. Choose Install app or Add to Home screen.
  4. Open YUVA Club from the new home-screen icon.
Open on Android
iPhone and iPad
  1. Open yuvaclub.app in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button.
  3. Choose Add to Home Screen.
  4. Open YUVA Club from the new home-screen icon.
