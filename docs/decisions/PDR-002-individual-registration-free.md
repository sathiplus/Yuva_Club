# PDR-002 - Individual Student Registration Remains Free

## Date

July 10, 2026

## Status

Accepted

## Volume / Chapter Affected

Student Platform, Business Model, Organization Platform, Billing, Master Administration.

## Decision

Individual student registration remains free at the foundation level.

## Reason

Free individual registration lowers the adoption barrier, supports the student-first mission, and allows students to build a lifelong YUVA ID even before joining an organization.

## Alternatives Considered

- Charge students directly for basic accounts.
- Require organization membership before a student can join.
- Allow only school or nonprofit-managed registration.

## Consequences

- Organizations become the primary paid customer for administration, reporting, private groups, branding, and scale.
- The platform must support individual students with no organization membership.
- The product must distinguish free individual capabilities from organization-enabled capabilities.

## Follow-Up Work

- Define individual student entitlements.
- Define organization membership upgrade path.
- Define billing rules for organization-paid features.
