# PDR-003 - Organizations Pay for Platform Capabilities

## Date

July 10, 2026

## Status

Accepted

## Volume / Chapter Affected

Business Model, Organization Platform, Master Administration, Billing, API, Database.

## Decision

Organizations pay for administrative capabilities, reporting, private groups, branding, mentor workflows, scaled rosters, and operational support.

## Reason

This creates a sustainable revenue model while preserving free foundational access for individual students.

## Alternatives Considered

- Advertising-supported product.
- Student-paid subscription as the primary revenue model.
- Fully free platform with no organization subscription path.

## Consequences

- Subscription plans must be configurable.
- Organization features must be permissioned by plan.
- Billing data must be separated from student-owned learning records.
- Organization offboarding must preserve student continuity.

## Follow-Up Work

- Define subscription plan tables.
- Define plan feature flags.
- Define organization renewal and suspension workflows.
