## 1. Acceptance of Terms

By using YUVA Club, creating an account, registering a student, activating a parent account, accepting an organization invitation, or accessing an administrator dashboard, you agree to these Terms of Service.

If you are a parent or guardian, you are responsible for reviewing these terms with the student you authorize to use YUVA Club.

## 2. Platform Purpose

YUVA Club is a youth leadership development platform. It supports student registration, presentation practice, topic selection, research submission, AI-assisted feedback, parent visibility, organization participation, certificates, volunteer-hour tracking, and administrative oversight.

## 3. Eligibility

The current registration workflow is designed for students ages 13 to 21. The form requires parent or guardian information and parent permission acknowledgement.

## 4. Account Types

YUVA Club currently supports:

- Student accounts.
- Parent accounts linked to student records.
- Organization administrator accounts created by invitation.
- One platform Master Admin account, initially admin@yuvaclub.app.

Organization administrators and mentors are not publicly self-registered through the website. Organization administrators are invited by the Master Admin and set their own passwords through secure invitation links.

## 5. Account Security

Users are responsible for keeping login credentials secure. Users may not share passwords, impersonate others, bypass permissions, access another user's account, or attempt to view records outside their assigned role.

YUVA Club may suspend, deactivate, or restrict access when needed for security, safety, abuse prevention, or platform operations.

## 6. Student Ownership and Organization Membership

The student owns the global YUVA identity, portfolio, certificates, presentation history, volunteer history, and learning records associated with that account.

Organizations own only organization-specific membership, assignments, groups, coaches, teachers, moderators, organization activities, and organization-specific permissions.

Removing a student from an organization does not delete the student's global YUVA account or historical achievement records.

## 7. Acceptable Use

Users must use YUVA Club respectfully, lawfully, and honestly. Users may not:

- Upload harmful, illegal, infringing, abusive, hateful, sexually explicit, exploitative, or unsafe content.
- Harass, threaten, shame, or bully others.
- Misrepresent identity, age, parent authorization, organization affiliation, or achievements.
- Attempt to bypass login, role, organization, CSRF, or other security controls.
- Interfere with the platform or other users' learning environment.
- Upload malware, spam, or automated abuse.
- Use AI feedback or platform content to harm, mislead, or unfairly judge students.

## 8. Student Content

Students may submit topics, research notes, sources, presentation outlines, prepared questions, and uploaded files. Students and parents should avoid submitting sensitive information that is not needed for learning.

YUVA Club may use student submissions to provide platform features, administrator review, AI-assisted feedback, certificates, portfolio summaries, and organization-specific learning support.

## 9. AI-Assisted Feedback

YUVA Club may use AI tools to help review research and support presentation practice when configured. AI feedback is informational and educational. It is not a substitute for adult judgment, mentor review, or platform administrator approval.

YUVA Club may decide not to apply AI output to official scores, certificates, ranks, or feedback.

## 10. Presentations, Recordings, and Events

Students may participate in presentations, live sessions, or online events. The registration form includes acknowledgement that sessions may be recorded for educational purposes.

The application includes protected Zoom meeting fields, but automated recording storage is not currently implemented in the codebase.

## 11. Certificates, Badges, Portfolio, and Volunteer Hours

YUVA Club may display or generate certificates, badges, ranks, presentation counts, volunteer or service hours, portfolio summaries, and progress information. These records are educational program records and may require administrator review.

YUVA Club may correct errors, revoke improperly issued certificates, or update records when needed to maintain accuracy.

## 12. Organization Rules

Organization administrators may access only records assigned to their organization. Organization administrators may not create Master Admins, change platform-wide settings, edit global content, access platform billing, manage global AI settings, or view records outside their assigned organization.

Organizations may invite, link, assign, activate, deactivate, transfer, or archive organization memberships, but may not delete the student's global YUVA identity through the organization interface.

## 13. Parent Access

Parent access is limited to students linked to that parent account. Parents may not attempt to access another student's records.

## 14. Communications

YUVA Club may send service-related emails, including registration notifications, account activation links, invitation links, password reset links, support messages, and administrative notifications.

## 15. Privacy

Use of YUVA Club is also governed by the YUVA Club Privacy Policy and related consent and safety policies.

## 16. Third-Party Services

YUVA Club may rely on third-party services for hosting, database storage, email, AI-assisted feedback, and online meetings. Current implementation references Azure hosting, Azure SQL support, OpenAI API configuration, and Zoom meeting fields.

## 17. No Professional Advice

YUVA Club provides educational leadership practice and learning support. It does not provide legal, medical, mental health, financial, or professional advice.

## 18. Platform Availability

YUVA Club may change, suspend, or discontinue features. The platform may experience downtime, maintenance, errors, or interruptions.

## 19. Termination or Suspension
