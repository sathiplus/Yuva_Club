# YUVA Club Platform Constitution

## Purpose

YUVA Club exists to help students grow into confident communicators, thoughtful leaders, collaborative peers, and lifelong learners through student-led learning, presentations, mentorship, AI-assisted coaching, service, reflection, and measurable progress.

## Permanent Principles

1. Students own their achievements.
2. Every student receives a lifelong YUVA ID.
3. Individual student registration remains free at the foundation level.
4. Organizations pay for administration, reporting, private groups, branding, and scale.
5. AI is a coach, never a judge.
6. Human review remains available for sensitive decisions.
7. Minor privacy, consent, least privilege, and auditability are core product requirements.
8. Organization data is isolated by default.
9. Configuration is preferred over hard-coded behavior.
10. The platform should be designed for global, multilingual, multi-organization growth from the beginning.

## Decision Guardrail

Any product, technical, AI, pricing, privacy, or operations decision that contradicts this constitution must be recorded in the Product Decision Record with the reason, alternatives considered, and approval status.
