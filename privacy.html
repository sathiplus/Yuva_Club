Privacy Policy

Student privacy comes first.

YUVA Club is designed to protect student identity, parent consent, learning records, organization data, and safety reports.

Policy Summary

YUVA Club collects only the information needed to operate student registration, learning activities, parent visibility, organization membership, safety, certificates, and support.

Student achievements belong to the student. Organization access is limited by role and scope. Sensitive actions should be audited as the platform evolves.

For privacy questions, contact support@yuvaclub.app.
