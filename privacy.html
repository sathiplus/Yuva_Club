Privacy Policy

Student privacy comes first.

This policy explains how YUVA Club handles student, parent, organization, learning, safety, and account information.

Legal review note: This policy is based on the current YUVA Club platform. Child privacy, education records, international access, retention, and recording practices require review by qualified legal counsel before relying on this page as final legal advice.

Overview

YUVA Club is a youth leadership development platform for student registration, presentations, research practice, AI-assisted feedback, certificates, volunteer-hour tracking, parent visibility, organization participation, and administrator review.

We do not sell student personal information.

Information We Collect

  • Student name, preferred name, date of birth, age, grade, school, city/state, email, phone number, and WhatsApp contact if provided.
  • Parent or guardian name, relationship, email address, and phone number.
  • Membership path, organization invitation or join code, learning interests, presentation goals, availability preferences, and suggestions.
  • Code of Conduct agreement, recording acknowledgement, and parent permission acknowledgement.
  • Login credentials stored as password hashes rather than plaintext passwords.
  • Research notes, sources, presentation outlines, prepared questions, uploaded research files, topic selections, safety reports, scores, attendance, certificates, badges, volunteer/service hours, portfolio summaries, and AI feedback summaries.
  • Security and audit data, including IP address, user agent, login attempts, session activity, role-based access checks, and administrative actions.

Parent, Student, and Organization Accounts

Parent accounts are used to view student records connected to that parent. Parent access is controlled by backend parent-student relationship checks.

Organization administrators are invited by the Master Admin and may manage only students assigned to their own organization. Organizations do not own a student's global YUVA identity, certificates, portfolio, presentations, volunteer history, or global account.

The Master Admin has platform-level access for administration, organization setup, security, configuration, reports, and audit review.

AI-Assisted Practice and Feedback

YUVA Club includes AI-assisted review for student research and presentation preparation when an OpenAI API key is configured. The AI Coach may review student-submitted research notes, sources, presentation outlines, prepared questions, selected topics, and related learning information.

AI feedback supports learning. It should not be treated as a final human judgment. Platform administrators may review AI output before applying official scores, ranks, certificates, or feedback.

Legal review note: Final AI vendor terms, data processing terms, retention settings, and parental disclosure language must be reviewed before relying on this section.

Uploads, Recordings, Certificates, and Portfolios

Students may upload research or presentation preparation files in supported formats, including PDF, PowerPoint, Word documents, and images. YUVA Club may store uploaded files for student and administrator review.

YUVA Club may generate or display certificates, badges, rank status, presentation records, volunteer/service hours, topic selections, research status, and portfolio summaries.

The registration form asks students and parents to acknowledge that sessions may be recorded for educational purposes. The codebase includes protected Zoom meeting fields, but automated recording storage is not currently implemented in the application.

Legal review note: Recording practices require separate review for consent, storage, access controls, retention, deletion, and state/country-specific recording laws.

How We Use Information

  • Register students and create student accounts.
  • Create parent accounts and connect parents to students.
  • Support organization membership and organization administrator workflows.
  • Provide student dashboards, topic selection, research submission, AI-assisted feedback, certificates, volunteer-hour tracking, and portfolio summaries.
  • Review registrations, attendance, presentations, safety issues, and administrative notes.
  • Send account, invitation, activation, password reset, support, and notification emails.
  • Protect accounts, enforce permissions, prevent unauthorized access, and maintain audit logs.

Cookies, Hosting, and Storage

YUVA Club uses session cookies for login, security, and account access. Session cookies may use protections such as HttpOnly, Secure when served over HTTPS, SameSite=Lax, and strict session mode.

The current codebase does not show Google Analytics or third-party advertising trackers. If analytics, pixels, advertising, heatmaps, or third-party tracking are added later, this policy should be updated before launch.

YUVA Club is hosted on Azure App Service. The platform supports Azure SQL storage when configured. Some current workflows also use server-side file storage for registration CSVs, JSON records, uploads, audit logs, and invitation delivery logs.

Sharing Information

YUVA Club may share information only as needed to operate the platform: with linked parents or guardians, assigned organization administrators, platform administrators, service providers that host or process platform information, or when needed for safety, legal obligations, or platform protection.

International Access

YUVA Club may be accessed from outside the United States. Platform data may be processed in the United States or other locations where YUVA Club's hosting and service providers operate.

Legal review note: International transfer language requires legal review before final publication.

Retention, Deletion, and Privacy Requests

YUVA Club retains student records, certificates, presentations, portfolio summaries, organization membership history, parent links, and audit logs as needed to operate the platform, preserve student achievements, maintain security, and support organization accountability.

Removing a student from an organization does not delete the student's global YUVA identity or historical achievement records.

Students, parents, or authorized representatives may contact support@yuvaclub.app to request access, correction, deletion, or other privacy assistance. Some records may be retained where needed for security, audit, legal, safety, certificate history, organization accountability, or platform integrity.

Legal review note: Final retention schedules and a formal privacy request workflow are not yet implemented in code.

Children's and Student Privacy

YUVA Club is designed for youth learning and collects parent or guardian information and consent acknowledgement during registration. Parents may request assistance by contacting support@yuvaclub.app.

Legal review note: Do not treat this page as a verified compliance statement for COPPA, FERPA, state privacy laws, school contracts, or international student privacy laws until reviewed by counsel.

Security

YUVA Club uses role-based access controls, CSRF protection for sensitive forms, session security settings, password hashing for activated accounts, login attempt tracking, organization isolation, and audit logging for sensitive actions. No system can guarantee perfect security.
