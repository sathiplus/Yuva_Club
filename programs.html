Yuva Club Programs

Yuva Club helps students grow through research, presentations, discussion, service, mentorship, and leadership challenges. Members belong to an age-aware program group and progress through a challenge-style leadership journey.

School Yuva

Ages 13-17. For school students building public speaking, research, leadership, discussion, confidence, and service habits.

College Yuva

Ages 18-21. For college-age members focused on advanced presentations, mentoring, innovation, entrepreneurship, career preparation, and community leadership.

Leadership Journey

Explorer to Mentor

Points and AI feedback can support growth, but level advancement requires admin, mentor, or judge approval so certificates and awards remain meaningful.

Explorer

Learn, participate, build confidence, and complete onboarding.

Speaker

Present topics, answer questions, improve communication, and submit research.

Leader

Lead discussions, collaborate with teams, support sessions, and demonstrate consistency.

Mentor

Guide new members, provide feedback, inspire others, and represent Yuva Club values.

Yuva Club

A youth leadership development platform that empowers students through research, presentations, discussion, critical thinking, and peer learning.
