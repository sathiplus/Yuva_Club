Monthly Challenge Types

Public Speaking

Present with confidence, clear voice, strong structure, and audience awareness.

Research Presentations

Use trusted sources, organize ideas, explain evidence, and answer questions.

Leadership Activities

Lead discussions, support peers, organize tasks, and practice responsibility.

Debate and Discussion

Team Challenges

Collaborate on a shared problem, project, or presentation.

AI Presentation Coaching

Use AI feedback as a practice tool while mentors approve official scores.

Scoring Rubric

Judges score each category from 1 to 10 for a total of 100 points.

ConfidencePoise, preparation, and presence.
Voice ClarityVolume, pace, pronunciation, and expression.
Research QualityAccuracy, sources, and depth of understanding.
OrganizationClear opening, body, transitions, and conclusion.
CreativityOriginal thinking, examples, and memorable delivery.
Visual PresentationSlides, props, images, or structure that supports learning.
Audience EngagementEye contact, questions, interaction, and listener awareness.
Question HandlingThoughtful answers and calm response under pressure.
LeadershipResponsibility, respect, encouragement, and initiative.
Time ManagementFinishing within the expected time and pacing well.

Recognition

Students can earn digital badges, certificates, leadership points, achievement awards, mentor recognition, and a digital leadership portfolio that grows over time.
